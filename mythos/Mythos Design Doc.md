# 1\. Concept

Lane combat card game (in the style of Solforge or Might and Magic Duel of Champions) with a city building aspect for resources (in the style of Xcom base planning).

# 2\. Basic Gameplay

## **2.1. Decks:**

Deck consists of 30 cards shuffled together. 

1\. Cards are either units or buildings.

a. Units occupy lane spaces and deal damage to each other or buildings on front of them.

b. Buildings occupy city spaces and effect either units, other buildings, or enemy buildings.

2\. Draw 5 cards at start of the game and one during each draw step.

a. Selective mulligan (in the style of Hearthstone)

b. If you cannot draw a card you:

i. Lose (TBD)

ii.Shuffle and continue the game (TBD)

**Turn Order:**

1\. Draw Step

2\. City Effects

3\. Spell Track Effects

4\. Battle Phase

5\. Summon Phase

6\. Build Phase

**Game Board Layout:**

Two 5x5 spaces, with a Headquarters (O) in each center space. Two 5 space lanes (L) in between for units to fight in. 5 card spaces for the Spell Track (S)

Buildings can only go in city spaces (X spaces) and units can only go in lane spaces (L spaces)

**Mechanics:**

• Draw cards

• Stat buffs for cards/buildings (Raw numbers)

• Effect buffs for cards/buildings (I.E. Deathtouch, Flying, Mobility, Siege, Range, Protection/

Armor)

• Resource generation

• Cost lowering Effects

• Unit Summons (How that would work I pictured would be building the building, then that building's text is something like "Pay x: Place Merlin into play in a space of your choice. Only one Merlin may be in play at any given time" which would allow the super powerful units to have a sort of undying-ness to them as befits their legendaryness)

**Effects/Keywords**

• **Range x**: Can attack units up to x lanes away on either side

• **Armor x**: When this unit is dealt damage, prevent x of it

• **Haste**: Can attack immediately after summoning

• **Elusive**: Can’t be targeted by spells or effects (enemy or friendly)

• **Siege x**: This creature gains \+x damage when attacking a structure.

• **Mobility x:** This creature can move up to x lanes in either direction

• **Triumph**: When a unit is destroyed in this unit’s lane, do x.

• **Immune:** All damage dealt to this creature is reduced to 0\.

• **City Influence**: Bonuses based on quantity and types of buildings owned, i.e. If you control 3 or more generators, this unit gains \+x where x is number of generators

# 3\. Building Ideas

## *General Idea – First Draft (Pre Mythos ideas)*

**HQ** – Central Building. Holds player’s HP, no adjacency bonuses

**Generator** – Produce resources, raise resource cap. Adj \= Bonus resources

**Fortifications** – Increase wall HP. Adj \= shield bonuses

**Engineers** – Repairs buildings adjacent to engineer building. Adj \= None

**Tesla Coils** – Deals damage to units in lane. Either expensive or hits friendlies. Adj \= none

**Training School** – Provides unit buffs based on adjacency but only in one direction.

Adj to Gen \= lower resource costs

Adj to Engineers \= Units heal hp each turn

Adj to HQ \= More HP, maybe more atk (?)

**Grand Cannon** \- Attacks hit first object in front of it. Can only deal damage to structures. Fires every other turn. If built on top of a generator then fires every turn.

**Foundry** – Reduces time taken to build buildings. Adj \= further reduces time taken

# 4\. Factions

**Nordic/Valhalla** – Big unit and lane presence. Big stompy units. Aggressive gameplay, low building presence. 

**Celtic/Tir na n’Nog** – Unit combos are a priority. Lots of building/unit interaction, buffs, and effects. Strategic combo gameplay. Units and buildings middle of road in terms of stats.

**Arthurian/Avalon** – Large building presence. Low attack, high health. Aims for end game where it can summon powerful buildings to turn the tide of battle.

**Greek/Elysium** – Placement oriented faction. Units are slightly below the curve by themselves but with adjacency bonuses they become very strong. Buildings follow suit.

# Essence Tree — First 30 Purchases Walkthrough

Player-experience walkthrough across the first ~25 Life Cycles. Prestige
unlocks when the player purchases their first Familiar (tier 2 generator,
~5K-15K mana, 20-30 min into first session). Generator tiers 3-7 are
gated behind Essence Tree nodes, creating a "grind resets → breakthrough
→ ride the wave → grind again" rhythm.

## Essence Formula

`floor(sqrt(total_mana_earned / 1000)) * (1 + essence_bonus)`

| Mana Earned | Essence |
|-------------|---------|
| 4K | 2 |
| 10K | 3 |
| 25K | 5 |
| 50K | 7 |
| 100K | 10 |
| 500K | 22 |
| 1M | 31 |

---

## Phase 1: Learning the Loop (Cycles 1-4, ~15 min each)

The player works with tiers 0-2 only. Each cycle: start → buy Heartmotes
→ Glyphs cascade → Familiars cascade → prestige. Short, punchy loops.
Each cycle earns 3-5 essence.

### Purchase 1: Head Start L1 — 2 essence
> +200 starting mana per cycle

Cheapest node in the tree. One glowing node amid dim silhouettes. Next
cycle starts with enough mana to buy a Heartmote immediately.

### Purchase 2: Mana Magnetism L1 — 3 essence
> +5% all production

The main Verdant trunk starts. Cascade Resonance appears dimly as the
next trunk node. Production across all generators is boosted.

### Purchase 3: Sanctum Mastery L1 — 4 essence
> +10% bloom bonus

Bloom branch opens. Deep Roots and Auto-Tend appear dimly. Three areas
of the tree are lit.

### Purchase 4: Vital Strength L1 — 4 essence
> +20% vitality earning rate

Four branches active. Endurance and Beat Enrichment appear in Vital.

### Purchase 5: Mana Magnetism L2 — 3 essence
> +10% all production total

Cheap repeat. The ring fills to 2/10.

**~16 essence spent. Player can see Warden Gate in the Verdant branch —
"I need 15 essence for that... a few more resets."**

---

## Phase 2: Building Toward Tier 3 (Cycles 5-9, ~15-20 min each)

The player grinds resets with a purpose: accumulate enough essence to
unlock Wardens. Meanwhile they buy production multipliers that make each
3-tier cycle richer. Each cycle earns 5-8 essence.

### Purchase 6: Surge Frequency L1 — 5 essence
> -10% surge cooldown

All five branches active. The tree looks like a star with one lit node
on every arm.

### Purchase 7: Head Start L2 — 2 essence
> +400 starting mana total

### Purchase 8: Mana Magnetism L3 — 3 essence
> +15% all production total

### Purchase 9: Endurance L1 — 6 essence
> Vitality expiry +6 hours (30 hr total)

Weekend walks carry into weekdays.

### Purchase 10: Cascade Resonance L1 — 8 essence
> +8% cascade echo chance

First trunk-2 node. Echo Amplifier and Tier Mastery appear deeper in
Verdant — the player can now see three levels deep. Random double-
production beats start happening.

### Purchase 11: Head Start L3 — 2 essence
> +600 starting mana total

### Purchase 12: Warden Gate — 15 essence
> UNLOCK TIER 3 GENERATORS (Verdant Wardens)

**First major breakthrough.** ~64 total essence across ~9 cycles. The
player has been grinding 3-tier loops, watching their tree grow, and now
tier 3 opens up. Wardens produce Familiars which produce Glyphs which
produce Heartmotes — the cascade deepens. Next cycle's mana output jumps
dramatically.

---

## Phase 3: Riding the Tier 3 Wave (Cycles 10-14, ~20-30 min each)

With Wardens online, each cycle produces way more mana → way more
essence. 10-15 essence per cycle with Essence Echo. The player invests
in mid-tree nodes while eyeing the Spire Gate.

### Purchase 13: Beat Enrichment L1 — 10 essence
> Each beat produces +3% more mana

The exercise amplifier. More heartbeats from movement (natural 1:1) AND
each beat is richer (tree bonus). Step Compounding appears as a side path.

### Purchase 14: Essence Echo L1 — 10 essence
> +10% essence earned per prestige

Compounds forever. Every future cycle is 10% richer. Swift Rebirth and
Cycle Momentum appear in Arcane. Highest-ROI node in the tree.

### Purchase 15: Sustained Pulse L1 — 10 essence
> Surge effects last 15% longer

Pulse trunk-2. Dual Surge and Surge Mastery appear.

### Purchase 16: Mana Magnetism L4 — 3 essence
> +20% all production

### Purchase 17: Deep Roots L1 — 10 essence
> +10% sanctum growth speed

Bloom trunk-2. Bloom Cascade and Eternal Garden appear.

### Purchase 18: Generator Memory L1 — 6 essence
> Start cycle with tier 1 unlocked

Huge QoL — skip the "save for first Pulse Glyph" grind every cycle.

### Purchase 19: Swift Rebirth L1 — 8 essence
> +50% production for first 5 minutes

Pairs with Head Start + Generator Memory for an explosive opening sprint.

**~57 essence in this phase. Three branches at trunk-2. Player is deeper
in the tree and can see the Spire Gate (35 essence) on the horizon.**

---

## Phase 4: The Spire Gate Grind (Cycles 15-18, ~30-40 min each)

The player can see the Spire Gate (35 essence). They need to save across
2-3 cycles, or keep buying smaller nodes while accumulating. Tension:
"do I grab Surge Mastery now, or save for tier 4?" Each cycle earns
15-20 essence.

### Purchase 20: Quick Recovery L1 — 6 essence
> Surge offer window +5 sec

### Purchase 21: Cascade Resonance L2 — 10 essence
> +16% cascade echo chance total

Scaling cost kicks in (was 8, now 10).

### Purchase 22: Beat Enrichment L2 — 12 essence
> Each beat +6% more mana total

### Purchase 23: Surge Mastery L1 — 18 essence
> +20% surge effect power

First trunk-3 node. Convergence Surge (trunk-4) appears dimly at the
branch tip.

### Purchase 24: Spire Gate — 35 essence
> UNLOCK TIER 4 GENERATORS (Heartfont Spires)

**Second major breakthrough.** ~81 essence in this phase, ~218 total.
The cascade is now 5 tiers deep. Production explodes. This should feel
like a massive reward for the patient grind.

---

## Phase 5: Deep Tree Investment (Cycles 19-25, ~40-60 min each)

With 5 tiers running, cycles produce massive mana. 20-40 essence per
cycle. The player fills out trunk-3 nodes, pushes side paths, and starts
eyeing the Aether Gate (60 essence) for tier 5.

### Purchase 25: Tier Mastery L1 — 20 essence
> +15% production for highest unlocked tier

Verdant trunk-3. Aether Gate and Infinite Growth appear — the player
sees the tier 5 unlock for the first time. "Wait, there are MORE
generator tiers?"

### Purchase 26: Essence Echo L2 — 10 essence
> +20% essence per prestige total

### Purchase 27: Bloom Cascade L1 — 16 essence
> Full bloom triggers production burst (+30 sec equivalent)

Blooms become exciting events, not just quiet resets.

### Purchase 28: Generator Memory L2 — 6 essence
> Start cycle with tiers 0-2 unlocked

Skips straight to Familiars at cycle start.

### Purchase 29: Endurance L2 — 8 essence
> Vitality expiry +12 hr total

### Purchase 30: Mana Magnetism L5 — 3 essence
> +25% all production. Halfway through Magnetism's 10 levels.

**~63 essence in this phase. Running total: ~281 essence across ~25
cycles. 15 of ~38 distinct nodes touched.**

## What Comes Next (Purchases 31-50)

### Tier Gate Chain (the big carrots)
- Aether Gate (60) — unlock tier 5 (Aether Conduits)
- Worldpulse Gate (90) — unlock tier 6 (Worldpulse Anchors)
- Primordial Gate (120) — unlock tier 7 (Primordial Hearts)

### Trunk-4 Capstones (50-80 each)
- Infinite Growth (50) — remove upgrade level caps
- Vital Bloom (55) — spend vitality on sanctum growth
- Overgrowth (60) — extra sanctum slots
- Convergence Surge (65) — surge + sanctum bridge
- Transcendence (80) — endgame prestige layer hook

### Deep Side Paths
- Cycle Momentum (14+) — scales with cycle count
- Memory Palace (20+) — retain upgrades through prestige
- Auto-Tend (20) — sanctum QoL
- Dual Surge (30) — two surges simultaneously

## Player Experience Phases

| Phase | Cycles | Run Length | Essence/Cycle | What's Happening |
|-------|--------|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| Learning the loop | 1-4 | 15 min | 3-5 | 3-tier loops, trunk-1 nodes |
| Grinding for T3 | 5-9 | 15-20 min | 5-8 | Saving toward Warden Gate |
| **Warden Gate** | ~9 | — | — | **Tier 3 unlocks, cascade deepens** |
| Riding T3 | 10-14 | 20-30 min | 10-15 | Mid-tree, trunk-2 nodes |
| Grinding for T4 | 15-18 | 30-40 min | 15-20 | Saving toward Spire Gate |
| **Spire Gate** | ~18 | — | — | **Tier 4 unlocks, production explodes** |
| Deep investment | 19-25 | 40-60 min | 20-40 | Trunk-3, eyeing Aether Gate |
| Endgame push | 25+ | 60+ min | 40-100+ | Tier gates 5-7, capstones |
| Mastery | 40-60 | Varies | Varies | Maxing everything, Transcendence |

The tier gates create natural "grind → breakthrough → ride the wave →
grind again" rhythm. Each gate is a memorable moment where the game
transforms. The tree drives the entire progression — not mana thresholds.
